Week 1

4 simple steps

  1. Think

    What does the customer want, what is needed (resources, knowledge, etc) to build it?

  2. Design

    Decide how it should look behave and interact

  3. Test

    Decide how it should look behave and interact

  4. Create

    Begin Building implement the above mentioned steps while testing for defects and or weekness's in steps 1-3

4 Design steps is much better then build it all the way out only to scream, oh know i have a bug and i don't know where it is!!
